Privacy Notice

HalluciNot processes uploaded files on its backend in order to extract and verify legal citations.

Citation lookup data is sent to CourtListener for verification. Do not upload sealed, privileged, or highly confidential documents unless you are comfortable with that workflow.

The service is intended to minimize permanent storage of uploaded documents, but users should treat the product as a cloud-based review tool rather than a private local desktop application.
